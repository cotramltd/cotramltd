ABOUT COTRAM LIMITED

Cotram Limited is a versatile import and export company strategically located in Kireka along the Kampala-Jinja highway. Specializing in international trade, we connect local markets with global suppliers and customers, ensuring seamless logistics and high-quality products. In addition to our import and export services, Cotram Limited offers exceptional catering solutions tailored to meet diverse needs, from corporate events to private gatherings. With a commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we are your trusted partner for trade and hospitality services in Uganda and beyond. Visit us in Kireka for professional and dependable services that drive success.
